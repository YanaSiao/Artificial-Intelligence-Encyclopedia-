GPT-3 was the model that proved "Scale is All You Need." It solidified the decoder-only architecture as the standard for generative AI.

- **Architecture:** 96 layers, 12,288 hidden dimension, 175 Billion parameters.
    
- **Computation:** Estimated at $3.14 \times 10^{23}$ FLOPS for training.
    
- **Dataset:** Roughly 300 billion tokens, including Common Crawl, WebText2, and Wikipedia.
    
- **Performance:** Achieved state-of-the-art results on many benchmarks without requiring a single weight update (zero-shot).
